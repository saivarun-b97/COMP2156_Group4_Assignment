GEORGE BROWN COLLEGE (BRANDED AS GEORGE BROWN POLYTECHNIC SINCE 2025)
================================================================================

Overview
--------
George Brown College is a public, fully accredited college of applied arts and
technology with three campuses in downtown Toronto: St. James, Casa Loma,
and the Waterfront. 
It offers a wide variety of programs in art and design, business, community services,
health sciences, and engineering technologies. 
The college is known for its focus on practical, hands-on learning and its strong
connections to industry partners, helping students prepare for successful careers in the Greater Toronto Area.


Key Features and Highlights
----------------------------

Location:
Three main campuses—St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront—sit in the heart of 
Toronto, providing direct access to key industries.

Academic Focus:
Known for hands-on, practical training in fields such as business, hospitality, 
culinary arts, health sciences, design, and technology.

Student Demographic:
A highly diverse, international environment with 30-44% of students coming from 
over 100 countries.

Industry Integration:
Programs are developed in consultation with industry partners, often featuring 
field education, co-ops, and placements.

Rankings & Reputation:
Ranked as one of Canada's top 10 research colleges (2022) and offers a 74-81% 
graduate satisfaction/employment rate.

Student Life:
Offers a vibrant community with over 50 clubs, competitive sports teams 
(George Brown Huskies), and specialized services for international students.


Summary
-------
The institution, which also provides a "direct-from-industry" approach, is 
considered a top choice for both local and international students seeking 
job-ready skills in a bustling urban setting.
