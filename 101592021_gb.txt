George Brown College (GBC) is located in Toronto, Canada. 
It offers practical, hands-on, online high quality programs in technology, business, and healthcare. 
GBC is not only located in one of the most leading cities but GBC allows for adaptative learning and industry partnerships to prepare students for any challenges they may face within themselves or in the outside world. The campus includes high tech labs, modernized classrooms, and collaborative learning spaces for students.
